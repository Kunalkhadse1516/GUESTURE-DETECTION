Specifications for Virtual Painter

If you are confused how to get started with the virtual painter feature provided by us, then first have a look at this guide “SPECIFICATIONS FOR VIRTUAL PAINTER” mentioned below and follow the steps as mentioned.

STEP 1: Click on the Virtual Painter button on the Home Screen.

STEP 2: A window will open which will have brush available in three colors (Red , Green and Blue) and one eraser.

STEP 3: User can draw with the help of two fingers and can select brush or eraser with one finger.

STEP 4: One thing to remember while drawing is that you have to keep your hand away from screen to clearly see what you have drawn.
