Hand Gesture and Live Painting

Virtual painter using AI is the real time feature included in hand gesture recognition where one can use their hand to draw on the screen.

In this, first it track our hand and get its landmarks and then use the points to draw on the screen. Two fingers are used for selecting the objects : like brush or pencil and one finger is used for drawing.

Deep learning, and computer vision have made a great impact on the lives of people who cannot hear or speak. Hand gesture recognition project will bridge the gap between normal people and the ones who cannot speak or hear. This will be very helpful for them as they can communicate with others through their hand gestures. Here gestures are converted in the form of text to be understood by common people.
