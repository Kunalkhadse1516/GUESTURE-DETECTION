Specifications for Hand Gesture Detection

If you are confused how to get started with the hand gesture detection feature provided by us, then first have a look at this guide “HAND GESTURE DETECTION” mentioned below and follow the steps as mentioned.

STEP 1: Click on the Hand Gesture Detection button on the Home Screen.

STEP 2: After this, camera will open in front of the user.

STEP 3: User have to adjust their hand in front of camera and do some gesture movement.

STEP 4: Then the gesture made by user is matched with the pre-defined dataset.

STEP 5: User will get to know the meaning of the hand gesture in the form of text on the screen.
